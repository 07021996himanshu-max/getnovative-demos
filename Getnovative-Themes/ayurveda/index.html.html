FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
▾
You can book through our website's appointment form, call us at +91-123456780, or WhatsApp us. We confirm appointments within 2 hours.
Do you offer painless root canal treatment?
▾
Yes! We use rotary endodontics and advanced local anaesthesia techniques. Most patients describe minimal to zero discomfort during the procedure.
What age is suitable for braces?
▾
Braces can be started from age 10-12 for children. Adults can get braces or invisible aligners at any age. We assess and recommend the best option during consultation.
Do you accept insurance?
▾
Yes, we work with most major dental insurance providers. Our team can assist with claims and insurance coordination, especially for international patients.
How long does a dental implant procedure take?
▾
The implant placement takes 1-2 hours. Total healing time is 3-6 months for the bone to integrate. Same-day temporary crowns are available.
Do you provide services for international patients?
▾
Absolutely! We have a dedicated dental tourism program. We arrange airport pickup, accommodation assistance, multilingual communication, and complete treatment planning for international visitors.
Is EMI available for expensive treatments?
▾
Yes, we offer flexible EMI options on treatments above ₹15,000. 0% EMI for 3-6 months is available. Ask our front desk team for details.
